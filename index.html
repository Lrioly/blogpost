Highlights from the Educational Tour 2025

Let us uncover what happened during the unforgettable week long

By Emmanuel John Nuñez

Day 1:

We kicked off our journey by visiting Foundever, where we got an inside look at how customer experience solutions are powered by innovation and teamwork. Later, we stepped into the world of one of the biggest tech giants — Google. From the vibrant office culture to the inspiring stories shared by Googlers, it was a day filled with motivation and digital marvels.

Day 2:

Day two took us to Teleperformance, a global leader in BPO services, where we discovered how tech and empathy blend in customer service. In the afternoon, we visited Kollab, a dynamic co-working space and community that fosters creativity, startups, and collaboration — a peek into the future of workspaces.

Day 3:

We explored two contrasting yet impactful organizations: the Asian Development Bank (ADB), where development meets strategy, and Microsourcing, which showcased the power of outsourcing and its role in business growth. Both visits broadened our view of economic and digital development.

Day 4:

Time to unwind! We headed to Sky Ranch in Tagaytay for a well-deserved day of leisure. Surrounded by cool weather and scenic views, the thrilling rides and shared laughter made this day a perfect break from the corporate buzz.

Day 5:

A full day in Baguio gave us a taste of heritage and beauty. We visited the Philippine Military Academy for a glimpse of discipline and tradition, explored the serene Bell Church, enjoyed the vibe at Burnham Park, took in the views at Mines View Park, and admired the historical Mansion House. A meaningful journey through the Summer Capital of the Philippines.

Day 6:

With a free schedule, we embraced spontaneity! We had fun at Timezone, revisited some favorite city spots, and explored more of Manila at our own pace. A laid-back end to a packed and purposeful trip.
